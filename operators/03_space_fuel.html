💡 The Multiplication Operator

In Python, multiplication uses the asterisk symbol * (shift + 8 on most keyboards). Do not use the letter 'x', as Python will think it is a variable!

Space Jumps: 4
Fuel per Jump: 10 L
4 * 10 = 40 Liters
⚙️ Your Task

Your rocket ship consumes a constant amount of fuel for each jump through hyper-space. Multiply the number of jumps by fuel per jump to calculate total fuel.
