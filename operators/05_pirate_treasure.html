💡 The Floor Division Operator

In Python, floor division is written with two forward slashes //. Unlike normal division, floor division divides the numbers and **rounds down** to the nearest whole integer, throwing away any remainder or decimal parts. It answers the question: *How many full, complete times does a number fit into another?*

Total Coins: 20
Number of Pirates: 4
20 // 4 = 5 coins each
