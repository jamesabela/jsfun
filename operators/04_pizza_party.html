💡 The Division Operator

In Python, normal division uses a single forward slash /. Normal division **always** returns a decimal number (a float), even if the numbers divide perfectly. For example, 12 / 4 outputs 3.0.

Total Slices: 12
Number of Players: 4
12 / 4 = 3.0 slices
