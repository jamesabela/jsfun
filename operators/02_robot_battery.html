💡 The Subtraction Operator

In Python, the subtraction operator is the hyphen -. It subtracts the number on its right from the number on its left.

Starting Battery: 100%
Battery Used: 25%
100 - 25 = 75%
⚙️ Your Mission

A tiny exploration robot is returning to the charging dock. It starts with some battery power and consumes a portion of it during its trip. Calculate how much battery is left. 

battery_left = start_battery - battery_used
