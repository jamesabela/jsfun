Presence Check

Level 5 of 10
💡 What this means

A presence check makes sure that data has been entered. It is useful when a field must not be left blank. In Python, an empty string is written as two quote marks with nothing between them.

🌍 Real-life examples
  • A sign-up form requires a username.
  • A delivery form requires an address.
  • A school survey may require a year group before it can be submitted.
  • A medical form may require an emergency contact number.
