💡 What this means

Good systems often combine several checks. Validation checks make sure data is acceptable. Verification checks help confirm copied data was entered accurately. The final program uses both ideas in one booking form.

🌍 Real-life examples
  • A school trip form may require a name, age range, booking code and matching email addresses.
  • An online shop may validate stock quantity, postcode format and presence of an address.
  • A bank form may use validation for number formats and double entry verification for account details.
  • A hospital form may use many checks because inaccurate data could cause serious problems.
