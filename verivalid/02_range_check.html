Range Check

Level 2 of 10
💡 What this means

A range check makes sure a number is within one or two limits. It is used when values outside the limits should not be accepted. In an algorithm, this is usually written with selection, or with a loop that keeps asking until the value is valid.

🌍 Real-life examples
  • An exam mark must be from 0 to 100.
  • A month number must be from 1 to 12.
  • A booking system may reject a date that is before today.
  • A game may only accept a difficulty level from 1 to 5.
