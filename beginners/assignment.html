LEVEL 4: CREATIVE TASK

Variable Assignment 🎨

💡 creative mission

Write your own variables and format output sentences dynamically! Have fun and be creative.

💡 No automated tests: This task is creative. You can run your code to see the output and click Complete Course 🎓 whenever you are ready!
📝 assignments checklist
Core Task Assign & Output
Assign your name and your favourite food to 2 separate variables with suitable names. Output the contents of the variables on 2 separate lines.
Extension 1 Create Sentences
Output two sentences (not just the contents of the variables). The first with your name, the second with your favourite food.
Extension 2 Combined Sentence
Output both pieces of information as part of the same sentence. Make sure that you have spaces and punctuation in the correct places.
