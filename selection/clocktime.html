LEVEL 8: SELECTION

12h to 24h Clock ⏰

⏰ Time Conversion

Let's build a program to convert standard 12-hour clock times (with am or pm) to their 24-hour equivalents!

💡 Hint: You don't have to change the minutes, just the hours. Ask for the hours, minutes, and period ("am" or "pm") separately.
🎯 Your Task

Write a program to ask for hours (1-12), minutes (0-59), and period ("am" or "pm"). Then print the converted 24-hour time in the format HH:MM.

Rules for conversion:

  • AM: If the hour is 12, it becomes 0 (e.g. 12:15 am -> 00:15). Otherwise, the hour stays the same.
  • PM: If the hour is 12, it stays 12 (e.g. 12:45 pm -> 12:45). Otherwise, add 12 to the hour (e.g. 7:30 pm -> 19:30).
⚙️ How Tests Work: The test runner checks three times:
• Inputting 7, 30, pm must output 19:30.
• Inputting 12, 15, am must output 00:15.
• Inputting 12, 45, pm must output 12:45.
📋 Original Code

Start building your clock converter here:

minutes = int(input("Minutes (0-59): "))
